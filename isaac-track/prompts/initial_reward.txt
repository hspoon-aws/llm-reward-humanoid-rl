GUIDANCE
--------
- This is the FIRST attempt. Start simple and physically grounded.
- The objective is reaching the Goal. A strong starting point rewards
  reducing the distance to the Goal (progress) and arriving within the
  success radius, while keeping the robot upright so it does not fall on the
  way. A robot that falls makes no progress, so include an upright/posture
  term and an alive bonus.
- Reasonable starting components: a goal-distance / progress-toward-goal term,
  an arrival bonus near the Goal, an upright/posture term, an alive bonus, and
  small penalties on action magnitude and joint velocity for smoothness.
- FACE THE GOAL — natural forward walking. A humanoid must walk FORWARD toward
  the Goal, facing its direction of travel; it must NOT walk backward or
  sideways. Distance-only rewards are gamed by backward shuffling, so you MUST
  include a heading/facing term: reward alignment between the robot's forward
  (+x base) axis and the direction to the Goal, and reward forward base
  velocity (root_lin_vel_b[:, 0] > 0) toward the Goal while penalizing negative
  (backward) and large lateral base velocity. Derive the robot's yaw from
  root_quat_w (w,x,y,z) and the goal bearing from (goal_xy - pos_xy); the
  facing term should be maximal when the robot points at the Goal and walks
  forward into it.
